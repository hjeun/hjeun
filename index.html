Research

My research focuses on temporal detection of actions, activities, and events in videos. Previously, I have also researched on detection of objects in several types of images (texts, salient objects, lung nodules, dental caries).


News

- Our team won the first place in Korean Text Detection in Traffic Road Signs of Samsung Fire & Marine Insurance Machine Learning Challenge 2017. We achieved 98.14% accuracy with 0.17 fps on 10k test images with 709 Korean text classes.
